Gymapp Fitness

Platform: iOS (App Store), Android (Google Play Store), PC (Windows/Mac)
Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Tools Used: Visual Studio, Photoshop, After Effects
Duration: 1 month
Completion: 2023
Role: Concept development, gameplay programming, graphic design,
UI development, testing, debugging and publishing



Gymapp Fitness is an app that helps you track your workouts and progress at the gym. Since it was developed with a handful of people's needs in mind, it might be missing features that others would find useful. That said, it includes many features that I think are great, and everyone can use it for free.
