Metroidvania Scene Demo

Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Tools Used: Visual Studio, Photoshop, After Effects
Duration: ~4 months
Completion: 2021

This is a 2.5D metroidvania scene demo I put together, and it’s packed with all sorts of features. The player controller has a rigid body, collision detection, movement, jump, dash, attacks, block, stunning, and parrying mechanics, along with a few other things. For the enemies, I built an AI system with patrolling paths, player detection, blocking and attacking behaviors, and even combo attacks. The combo attacks are visually appealing and add just the right amount of difficulty without making things unfair —like something you'd expect from an elite enemy, just below a boss fight.

The scene also features smooth camera work inspired by Hollow Knight, like locking vertically at the base level or horizontally at the edges. I added music and sound effects, particle systems and visual effects for attacks, environmental details and blood particles, programmatic sprite animations, UI elements, and more. It was a huge project I worked on a few years ago as a way to learn the Unity API and Engine features, but I had a lot of fun figuring out how all the pieces fit together.
